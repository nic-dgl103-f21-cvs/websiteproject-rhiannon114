Come Join Us

Are you between the ages of 12-18 and looking for something to do Tuesday night? If so drop by the end of Goose spit and find out what sea cadets is all about. We parade ever Tuesday night at 6pm and rap thing us by 9pm.

News and Events

At the end October we had a small Halloweeen party. The were encoraged to come out in their costumes and took part of a number of spokey activities. One of the favourite activities of the night was the pinata filled with candy that was gracously donated by CI Lynxleg for all to enjoy. Our Corp Halloweeen party has started to become a bit of a tradition that we hope will continue on in the up comeing years.

Our Location

We are currently on HMCS Quardra at the end of Goose Spit. When you arrive at at the gate the commisioner will give you directions for you to find the cadets buildings.
